# Commercial Licensing

ApiWright is free for personal and other noncommercial use under the
[PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0](LICENSE).

Using ApiWright for a commercial purpose—including internal business use,
commercial services, paid products, or work performed for a customer—requires
a separate paid commercial license.

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Commercial use | License validation |
|------|-------|----------------|--------------------|
| Free | 0 € | No — personal and other noncommercial use only | None, no key |
| Pro | 12 € per user / month, billed monthly, cancel anytime | Yes | Key, validated against the hosted ApiWright license server |
| Enterprise | Custom (volume pricing, invoicing) | Yes | Key, validated against a license server hosted in your own infrastructure |

Commercial teams can evaluate ApiWright free of charge for 60 days: start the
trial under **Help → License & Billing** — no key or account required. After
the trial, commercial use requires a Pro or Enterprise license.

Activate a key in the app under **Help → License & Billing**. A validated
license is cached locally, so ApiWright keeps working offline until the end of
the paid period.

To buy Pro seats or request Enterprise terms, contact the repository owner
through the GitHub profile or repository contact channels. Commercial rights
are granted only by a separate written agreement.
